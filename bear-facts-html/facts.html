Bears

Bear Facts

Their scientific name is "Ursidae." The Polar Bear can reach speeds of up to 25 mph. The Giant Panda has a 20 year life span.

Sizes of some bears:

  1. 150 – 220 lbs (Giant panda)
  2. 990 lbs (Polar bear)
  3. 240 lbs (American black bear)
  4. 180 – 1,300 lbs (Brown bear)
